"Why should we worry about the negative impact fast fashion has?"

Fast fashion has an enormous environmental footprint for both its
production and disposal.
Clothing production requires a
considerable amount of energy
and resources, while it depends on
toxic fabric dyes and other
chemicals that contaminate fresh water.
