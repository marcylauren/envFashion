Alternatives to fast fashion include sustainable and ethical fashion, which emphasizes eco-friendly materials, fair labor practices, and reduced waste. Slow fashion, a prominent alternative, focuses on creating high-quality, timeless pieces that are meant to last, rather than trend-driven items. Thrift shopping and buying second-hand clothing are also popular alternatives, allowing consumers to reduce waste.

What is thrifting?

Thrifting is the act of shopping for second-hand or pre-owned items, typically clothing and household goods, at thrift stores, garage sales, or online platforms. It is often pursued for its cost-effectiveness, unique finds, and environmental benefits through recycling and reducing waste.
